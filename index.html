About


"Phantom & the Blud" is a dynamic rap duo, but it also is more than just a rap duo; it's a creative force that transcends the boundaries of music. Comprising Nial, known as OrangePhantom, and Vikrant Shetty, aka BLU BLUD, this dynamic duo brings a unique blend of talent and vision to the world of hip-hop. With Nial's groovy beats and producing prowess and Vikrant's razor-sharp rhymes and storytelling, their music is a captivating fusion of artistry and authenticity. Welcome to the world of "Phantom & the Blud," where every track is a chapter in the story of life, art, and the relentless pursuit of dreams.



"Phantom & the Blud" started when two young friends, Nial and Vikrant, recognized their unique talents. Nial was tech-savvy, while Vikrant excelled in rap. They decided to merge their skills and create music together. Their partnership is driven by a shared passion for making meaningful music that connects with their audience, and that's how "Phantom & the Blud" was born.



Their creative journey is a testament to the power of collaboration and friendship, where two individuals, with their unique talents and backgrounds, come together to create something truly exceptional. As they continue to push the boundaries of rap and music as a whole, "Phantom & the Blud" invites you to join them on this artistic adventure, where every track is a new chapter in their evolving story.

OrangePhantom
(Nial George)

Nial, at just 13 years old, is the hidden genius behind the scenes of Phantom & the Blud. As the beat producer and mixer, his musical talents shine through, crafting the mesmerizing soundscape that accompanies the group's artistry. Despite his young age, he possesses a deep understanding of music production and a remarkable ability to blend various elements into harmonious compositions. His work adds a unique dimension to Phantom & the Blud's creations, elevating their music to new heights. While he may be the guy behind the scenes, his contributions are far from unnoticed, as his talent leaves an indelible mark on the group's sonic journey.



BLU BLUD
(Vikrant Shetty)

Vikrant plays a pivotal role as the singer-songwriter within Phantom & the Blud. With a profound connection to music and an innate ability to craft compelling narratives through song, he weaves intricate tales and emotions into his lyrical compositions, delivering a raw and authentic experience to the audience. His melodic prowess harmonizes seamlessly with the group's sonic tapestry, infusing each track with a distinctive and soul-stirring quality. Through his artistry, BLU BLUD brings forth a diverse range of emotions, making each song a powerful narrative that leaves an enduring impact on the collective journey of Phantom & the Blud.

Bound for Rebound





"Bound for Rebound" is a song that conveys a message of resilience, personal growth, and determination. Through its lyrics, the song encourages listeners to face life's challenges head-on and to overcome adversity. It suggests that setbacks and obstacles should not deter one's pursuit of their goals. Instead, the song urges individuals to rise above negativity, reject complacency, and strive for self-improvement. There's also a critique of certain elements of the music industry, highlighting the contrast between genuine artistry and mainstream success.
